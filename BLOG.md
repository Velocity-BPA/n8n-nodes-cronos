# Automate Cronos Blockchain Operations with Our New n8n Node

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-cronos, a powerful community node that brings Cronos blockchain automation directly into your n8n workflows!

## The Challenge

Blockchain developers and crypto enthusiasts working with Cronos have long faced a tedious challenge: manually monitoring accounts, tracking transactions, interacting with smart contracts, and managing DeFi positions. These repetitive tasks consume valuable time that could be spent on strategic initiatives and innovation.

## Enter n8n-nodes-cronos

Velocity BPA has developed this comprehensive node to bridge the gap between Cronos blockchain and n8n's powerful automation platform. Whether you're building a portfolio tracker, automating DeFi strategies, monitoring smart contract events, or integrating blockchain data into your business workflows, this node has you covered.

## Key Features

Our Cronos node provides seamless access to:

- **Account Management**: Query balances, track wallet activities, and monitor multiple accounts simultaneously
- **Transaction Operations**: Send, receive, and monitor transactions with real-time updates
- **Smart Contract Integration**: Interact with deployed contracts, call functions, and listen for events
- **Token Operations**: Manage CRC-20 tokens, check balances, and automate token transfers
- **DeFi Capabilities**: Connect with popular DeFi protocols on Cronos for yield farming, staking, and liquidity management

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically receiving Slack notifications when a specific wallet receives funds, triggering accounting workflows when transactions occur, or rebalancing your DeFi portfolio based on custom conditions—all without writing complex scripts or maintaining infrastructure.

## Getting Started

Installation is simple. Just run:


npm install n8n-nodes-cronos


Then restart your n8n instance, and you'll find the Cronos node ready to use in your workflow editor.
